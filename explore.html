Hey! It's nice to meet you. I graduated from the University of California, San Diego with a B.S. in NanoEngineering, and a minor in Chemistry in 2019. Throughout my years as an undergraduate, I primarily worked on product development in biotech. Post graduation, I worked as a researcher in medicinal chemistry.

Since then, I got my CAPM (Certified Associate in Project Management) certification and worked as a project manager to increase product adoption and guide ML initiatives.

Now, I'm studying Computer Science at Oregon State University.

As you might have noticed (or experienced it for yourself), passions change.
