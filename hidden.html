For the longest time, I was certain that my passion was in research.

As you might have noticed (or experienced for yourself), passions change.

A quick glance at my background might suggest that I too, am simply bound by the laws of entropy, drifting towards disorder.

However, I'm here to clarify.

I was captivated by science and worked in the research & development of treatments for: cancer, cystic fibrosis, and pain.
Every experiment revealed more information, allowing us to adapt our strategy and get closer to changing the lives of patients.

Likewise, with each company I worked at, each project I advanced, and each team I collaborated with, I learned about new technologies and developed transferrable skills.
While there are multiple variables, one constant is this: I have a bias for action.

Driven by curiosity and a desire for growth, I am not afraid to take on new challenges.
